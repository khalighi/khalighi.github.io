# The Inflection Point

**Date: Mar 14, 2026**

Software development is about to change forever. And most people don't see it yet.

For decades, we've been playing the same game—buy software, integrate it, customize it, maintain it. Hire engineers to fill the gaps. It was slow, expensive, and full of compromise. We accepted it because there was no alternative.

There is now.

AI agents are making it faster and cheaper to build exactly what you need than to adopt what already exists. The build vs. buy decision—the one that's shaped technology strategy for a generation—has flipped. Permanently.

But here's what I keep thinking about. The real opportunity isn't building faster with agents. It's building the platforms agents operate on.

Think about it. Agents are powerful. But they're unpredictable. They need guardrails. Compliance. Security. Reliability. Someone has to build the environment where agents can be trusted to operate—in regulated industries, in critical systems, in high-stakes decisions.

That's the platform worth building. Not another app. Not another integration. A foundation that makes AI trustworthy at scale.

That's defensible. That's a moat. And that window is open right now.
